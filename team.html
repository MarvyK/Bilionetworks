MEET THE TEAM

MARK AJHAM : CEO

Mark Ahjam is the Chief Executive Officer and founder of Bilionetworks. Mark provides strong technical and operational leadership to the organisation and also has a strong ethos of safety first and being agile. Mark brings a philosophy based on ethics and transparency which he believes strengthens trust among employees, customers and the community.

After completing his Electrical Engineering Diploma, Mark started his career with Plessey in 1989 as a Telecoms technician. In 1997, he left Plessey and co-founded BM Telecoms with branches in Cape Town and Gauteng. Mark's vision and passion for service excellence helped build BM Telecoms to a significant player in the ICT sector and, in 2009, Mark sold BM Telecoms to Sizwe IT where he managed the cabling department. He later left Sizwe IT to start Bilionetworks.

GUSTAV GRIESSEL : HEAD OF
STRATEGY & BUSINESS
DEVELOPMENT

Gustav is an experienced, values-based business leader with more
than 25 years senior management experience in the global commodities,
energy and resources industries. He has the ability to convert
strategy into actionable plans, delivering bottom-line results,
including continuous business improvement. He is responsible for
Strategy and Business Development and is always looking for
opportunities to grow the Company and add value for stakeholders.

Gustav completed B. Com Law and B. Com Hons degrees, with Law
and Economics as majors. He is also an accredited Semco Style
Master Consultant.

MELISSA WILLIAMS : HR MANAGER

Melissa Williams is the HR Manager at Bilionetworks. Melissa believes in leading HR through building trust and delivering services to all at Bilionetworks with speed and diligence. Her aim is to ensure that all employees are treated with respect, dignity and fairness in all processes implemented.

Melissa completed her Master of Commerce Degree specialising in Industrial Psychology. She has corporate experience in the HR field working in various industries like ICT, Finance and Education. Melissa's goal at Bilionetworks is to make sure that the best talent is employed and providing developmental opportunities to enhance the skillset at Bilionetworks and, in turn, enabling a platform for performance to thrive.

STEPHAN HOUGH : CFO

Stephan Hough is the CFO at Bilionetworks. Stephan's philosophy
to a well aligned Financial department is to lead with integrity
resulting in continuous improvement in all elements of the business
and utilising limited resources wisely.

Stephan has 15 years' experience working in Finance. He completed
his Bachelor of Commerce degree in Accounting at the University of
Stellenbosch. He is a professional member of the South African
Institution of Professional Accountants (SAIPA) and a member of the
Management Level of Chartered Institute of Management Accountants.
Ultimately Stephan's role is to lead the company in financial
performance by highlighting an possible risk and providing solutions
thereto.

DALE KLEINHANS : HEALTH &
SAFETY MANAGER

Dale Kleinhans is the Health and Safety Manager at Bilionetworks. Dale provides safety leadership through the provision of a safe work environment for all employees and the promotion of safety as a value. Dale's philosophy is that the employees of Bilionetworks are the most valuable resources of the business and that their safety is important. Dale is a strong believer in safety forming part of everyone's daily jobs.

Dale completed his SAMTRAC qualification. He worked in Health & Safety for almost a decade. He started his career in the construction industry as a Health & Safety Officer. He then became a consultant where he worked for various companies in various industries. Ultimately, Dale's role is to provide safe working practices and mitigate the risks at Bilionetworks.
