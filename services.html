SERVICES

OSP

 

Outside Plant Construction

OSP - building of civil fibre optic infrastructure, this includes:
- Way leave applications/approvals, route surveys, planning and quotations.
- Project Management.
- Mechanical and labour intensive trenching.
- Micro Trenching.
- Civil builds and duct network construction.
- Access layer and last mile routes.
- Directional drilling and thrust boring/impact moling.
- Pavement construction.
- Building and fitting manholes.
- Fibre floating, splicing and testing – FOA certified technicians.
- Site surveys and compiling detailed work scopes.
- Maintenance and SLA’s.

ISP

 

Inside Plant Construction

ISP – site builds for MDU’s and SDU’s
- Landlord approvals, route surveys, site planning and quotations.
- Multi dwelling (MDU), single dwelling (SDU) and business access builds.
- Fibre floating, splicing and testing – FOA certified technicians.
- Maintenance and SLA’s.
- Project Management.
